About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at The University of Texas at Austin, where my research spans Bayesian nonparametrics, clinical trial design, and generalized linear models. I am co-advised by Dr. Paul Rathouz and Dr. Peter Müller Before joining UT, I worked with Dr. Yuan Ji on innovative Bayesian trial designs. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Beijing Normal University.
